## Tier 1 Journals (High Impact)

### Physical Review Letters (PRL)

**Appropriate for:** Decoherence paper, Bell correlations paper
**Why:** Short format matches paper length, experimental predictions, high visibility
**Considerations:** 
- Very selective (~20% acceptance)
- Need clear falsifiable prediction
- Editor may question non-affiliated author

**Approach:**
- Lead with experimental prediction
- Emphasize sign difference from Diosi-Penrose (opposite to established model)
- Include specific protocols with existing missions (BECCAL, STE-QUEST)

### Nature Physics

**Appropriate for:** Full framework paper (if major result confirmed)
**Why:** Unification claim, cosmological implications
**Considerations:**
- Requires breakthrough-level result
- Better approached after experimental confirmation
- Pre-submission inquiry recommended

### Physical Review D

**Appropriate for:** Full framework, cosmology paper
**Why:** More space for derivations, gravity/cosmology focus
**Considerations:**
- More thorough review process
- Better for longer papers
- Good for establishing priority

## Tier 2 Journals (Solid Impact)

### Classical and Quantum Gravity

**Appropriate for:** Decoherence paper, gravity derivation
**Why:** Exact scope match, respects theoretical work
**Considerations:**
- Good balance of rigor and openness
- Strong quantum gravity community readership

### Foundations of Physics

**Appropriate for:** Bell correlations, interpretation papers
**Why:** Open to foundational questions
**Considerations:**
- Smaller readership but engaged
- Good for philosophical implications

### Physics Letters B

**Appropriate for:** Short theoretical notes
**Why:** Fast publication, good visibility
**Considerations:**
- Need very focused result

### European Physical Journal C

**Appropriate for:** Framework papers
**Why:** Open access, reasonable rigor
**Considerations:**
- Good for establishing record

## Response to Common Objections

### "No institutional affiliation"

*Response:* Independent researchers have a long history in physics (Einstein 1905, Ramanujan). The mathematics is either correct or incorrect regardless of affiliation.

### "AI collaboration"

*Response:* The AI served as a mathematical verification tool, similar to Mathematica or Maple. All theoretical ideas and physical interpretations are human-generated.

### "Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence"

*Response:* We provide falsifiable predictions. The claim is not that the theory is correct, but that it makes testable predictions distinguishable from existing models.

### "This contradicts established physics"

*Response:* We recover Newton, Schwarzschild, and Bell correlations exactly. The framework does not contradict established results — it provides a substrate interpretation for them.
