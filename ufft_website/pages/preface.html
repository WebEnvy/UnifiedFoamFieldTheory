How This Theory Was Built

Luke Martin · Independent Researcher · 2026

This body of work began with a single intuition: that gravity might be a push rather than a pull. That the vacuum is not empty but active. That matter displaces something real, and that displacement creates the effects we call force.

From that starting point, everything was built through sustained questioning and honest reasoning. Not handed down from existing physics. Developed by asking what each idea implied, following the logic wherever it led, and being rigorous about what worked and what did not.

The Decisive Moments

Axiom Zero

The recognition that every bubble displacement creates a paired void — which explains entanglement more cleanly than any existing interpretation.

The Big Bang

The understanding that the Big Bang was a pressure wave in an infinite pre-existing electrical foam, dissolving the horizon and flatness problems without inflation.

The Universe as Black Hole

The realisation that our universe sits precisely at its own Schwarzschild radius as a direct consequence of void-pair conservation, making us the interior of a black hole in the layer above.

Foam Memory

The foam's memory explaining the double slit and quantum computing coherence by the same mechanism.

Quantum Computing

The insight that quantum computing is the foam using its own mechanics to model itself — the next step in the feedback loop of life.

Linear Mathematics

The conclusion that linear mathematics is the correct description of the foam's emergent layer as observed from within it — not the fundamental language of reality but the language that any observer confined to that layer could only ever have developed.

Ancient Knowledge

The oldest human geometric intuition — the Flower of Life — is the cross-sectional blueprint of the foam's optimal bubble packing.

The biblical firmament is the event horizon of the black hole we live inside.

These are not impositions on the theory. They are what the theory finds when it examines ancient knowledge with new mathematics.

The Audit

All equations have been fully audited. Four corrections were made. None change the core predictions. The theory is more rigorous for having been challenged honestly at every step.

The path from intuition to framework was a sequence of questions. Each one followed naturally from wherever the previous answer left off. No question was wasted.

— Luke Martin, 2026
