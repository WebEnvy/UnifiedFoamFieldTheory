PART VI

The Correspondence Principle

How UFFT Degrades to Linear Mathematics

Luke Martin

Independent Researcher | 2026

For any physical theory to be viable it must reproduce established theories in their domains of validity. UFFT is built on non-linear foam dynamics. Standard physics is predominantly linear. This requires that the foam's non-linearity disappears at scales where linear physics has been verified, and survives only where current theories are already known to fail.

I. Gravity: Non-Linear → Newton [Proven]

The full foam acceleration: a = -(GM/r²) · 1/(1 - 2GM/rc²). Taylor expanding for x = 2GM/rc² << 1: the leading term is exactly Newton's law. Higher terms are suppressed by powers of x.

✓ At Earth's orbit: x = 1.97e-8. Error from using Newton instead of full expression = 1.97e-8 = 20 parts per billion. Well below any classical measurement precision.

The foam degrades to Newton with mathematical precision in the weak field limit. This is not approximate. It is exact in the limit x → 0.

II. Quantum Mechanics: Non-Linear Foam → Linear QM [Qualitative argument]

A quantum measurement at scale lambda involves a foam region containing:

N_bubbles ~ (lambda / l_Planck)^3 (1)

Non-linear bubble interactions average out over this ensemble. The suppression of non-linear corrections:

epsilon ~ (l_Planck / lambda)^2 (2)

✓ At atomic scale (lambda = 1e-10 m): epsilon = (1.62e-35 / 1e-10)^2 = 2.6e-50 ✓ At quantum optics scale (lambda = 1e-9 m): epsilon = 2.6e-52

Non-linear corrections to quantum mechanics are suppressed by 10⁻⁵⁰ at atomic scales. This is consistent with why no deviation from linear quantum mechanics has ever been observed --- the deviation is 50 orders of magnitude below any conceivable measurement sensitivity.

~ This argument is qualitatively sound and consistent with established literature on emergent quantum mechanics (Adler 2004, Zurek 2003). It is not a full derivation of the Schrödinger equation from foam mechanics --- that is open work.

III. Where Non-Linearity Legitimately Survives

A. Near the Schwarzschild Radius [Proven]

As r → r_s = 2GM/c², the non-linearity parameter x = 2GM/rc² → 1. The Taylor expansion fails. The vacuum density approaches zero --- the foam ceases to exist at r_s. This is precisely where GR and quantum mechanics are known to be incompatible. The foam predicts that the breakdown of linear physics near a black hole is a feature --- the full non-linear substrate is required and linear quantum field theory is insufficient.

✓ Divergence of acceleration at r_s confirmed analytically.

B. At the Planck Scale [By definition]

At lambda = l_Planck, epsilon = 1. Non-linear corrections are of the same magnitude as linear terms. The thermodynamic averaging argument fails completely. Full non-linear foam dynamics operate. This is the regime of quantum gravity --- where all current theories are inadequate. UFFT predicts non-linearity precisely where non-linearity is expected.

C. At Ultra-High Energies [Quantified, currently undetectable]

epsilon_energy ~ (E / E_Planck)^2 (3)

✓ LHC energy / Planck energy = 1.06e-15. Non-linear correction at LHC = 1.13e-30. Completely undetectable but precisely defined.

At energies approaching the Planck scale, deviations from linear quantum field theory should appear. Current accelerators are 15 orders of magnitude below this threshold. Future Planck-scale experiments would test this prediction directly.

IV. The Correspondence Summary

Three non-linearity parameters, each small wherever linear physics works, each approaching 1 precisely where current physics fails:

epsilon_gravity = 2GM/(r*c^2)
[small in weak fields] 
epsilon_quantum = (l_Planck/lambda)^2
[small at scales >> l_Planck] 
epsilon_energy = (E/E_Planck)^2
[small at sub-Planck energies]

UFFT is non-linear at its foundation and linear in all the places where linear physics works. The non-linearity survives precisely where current physics already knows it must.
